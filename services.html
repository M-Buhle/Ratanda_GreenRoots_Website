Our Programs and Services

Ratanda GreenRoots offers a variety of programs designed to uplift the community and promote sustainability.

Community Gardening Projects

We organize and maintain urban gardening projects, offering workshops and resources to residents who want to grow their own produce. This promotes food security and healthy living.

Our Services

Youth Development Programs

Our programs engage the youth of Ratanda Township through environmental education, coding workshops, and practical skill development, preparing them for future employment opportunities.

Recycling Initiatives

We run a local recycling collection and educational program to reduce waste, clean up the community, and promote environmental stewardship.
