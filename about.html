About Ratanda GreenRoots Community Garden

Our Overview

Ratanda GreenRoots is a community-based non-profit organisation located in Ratanda Township, Gauteng. We are committed to fostering environmental sustainability and community development.

Our History

Ratanda GreenRoots was proudly founded in 2023 to address pressing environmental and youth unemployment challenges within the Ratanda community. Since then, we have grown into a central hub for sustainable agriculture and skill-building.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission: To empower community members through practical gardening initiatives, comprehensive recycling schemes, and dedicated youth development programs.

Our Vision: To expand our gardening sites, significantly increase youth involvement, and cultivate small micro-businesses centered around fresh produce, leading to a greener Ratanda Township with active community participation.
